What is Camberley Book Buddies?

Camberley Book Buddies is a book club that started in Surrey, England.

Our members love of books is what unites us all and we wanted to expand so we could share that enjoyment with as many people as possible.

Within our library you can find books read by our users and read their reviews.

You can even add your own! Welcome to the club.
